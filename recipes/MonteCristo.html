Monte Cristo

Description

This is an elite sandwich. Imagine a solid ham and cheese sandwich, but the bread is french toast. Brazy

Ingredients

  • Brioche Sliced Bread
  • Honey Ham
  • Jerk Turkey
  • Honey
  • Gruyere
  • Muenster
  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Spread mayo on one side of a bread slice. Put the ham, turkey, and cheeses on the other side
  2. Beat egg with milk until combined. Heat a skillet under medium heat. Lightly oil
  3. Dip sandwich into egg mixture. Fry on skillet with butter until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Serve. Dress with honey and/or raspberry jam.
